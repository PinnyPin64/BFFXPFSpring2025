What is DefaultVeg? 🌿

DefaultVeg is a campaign that helps make plant-based food the default option at campus events—without removing choice. By making this small switch, your department can contribute to big wins for sustainability, inclusion, and health.

What about food allergies and religious dietary needs? ▼

DefaultVeg enhances inclusivity by:

  • Prioritizing naturally allergy-friendly bases (e.g., rice bowls)
  • Clear labeling of top allergens (soy, nuts, gluten)
  • Always providing an alternative upon request (halal, kosher, etc.)

🎓 For Students & Attendees

Will I still be able to get meat/dairy if I want it? ▼

Yes! DefaultVeg simply changes the default option—you can always request traditional meals. Most campuses report that only 10-20% of attendees opt for meat when plant-based is the default.

💡 Behavioral insight: Harvard found that making plant-based the default increased uptake by 300% without reducing satisfaction.
